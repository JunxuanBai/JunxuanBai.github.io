Short Bio

I am currently working for China Mobile Research Institute (CMRI) as a researcher.

I got my Ph.D degree in computer science from Beihang University in 2021. My advisors were Prof. Qinping Zhao and Prof. Junjun Pan. Before that, I received my M.S. degree in computer science from Beihang University in 2015 and B.S. degree in mathematics from Dalian Maritime University in 2012. My research interests include computer animation, 3D human motion, human emotion and all other relevant topics in computer graphics and virtual reality.

In the Useful Links, I collect some websites for computer graphics and VR.
