Short Bio

I am currently a Ph.D Candidate of Computer Science at Beihang University and Research Affiliate at the State Key Laboratory of Virtual Reality Technology and System. My supervisor is Prof. Qinping Zhao and my co-supervisor is Associate Professor Junjun Pan. Also I am in long-term collaboration with Prof. Hong Qin (Stony Brook University). I graduated with a Master Degree in Computer Science at Beihang University in June 2015.

My research interests include computer animation, human movement, human emotion, and visualization.

如果你对计算机图形学、VR/AR感兴趣，欢迎关注我们的知乎专栏“聊聊Computer Graphics”。
